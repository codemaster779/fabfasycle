Our Process

1. Collect Waste

Discarded fabrics and old clothes are sourced locally.

2. Design

We create inclusive designs (XS–XXL) that reduce waste.

3. Stitch

Local tailors repurpose fabrics into stylish, durable outfits.

4. Deliver

Your sustainable fashion arrives with a positive impact.

Our Impact

Environmental

Reducing landfill waste, pollution, and carbon emissions by repurposing textiles.

Social

Creating jobs for local tailors, supporting skill development, and spreading sustainable fashion awareness.

Economic

Affordable clothing that boosts the economy and supports communities while aligning with SDGs.
