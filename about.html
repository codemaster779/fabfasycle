Who We Are

Fabfasycle is an upcycled clothing line for both men and women (XSâ€“XXL), creating stylish and eco-friendly fashion from discarded textiles such as old clothes and fabric scraps. Our designs combine high-quality craftsmanship with sustainability, ensuring durability without compromising on style. Beyond repurposing fabrics, we also explore innovative fiber sourcing by converting food waste like sugarcane, orange peel, banana, and pineapple into unique, eco-conscious fabrics for our clothing.

Our Impact

Fabfasycle creates meaningful change on multiple levels. Socially, we generate jobs for local tailors, promote skill development, and spread awareness about sustainable fashion. Environmentally, we help reduce landfill waste, pollution, and carbon emissions by repurposing discarded fabrics. Economically, we support local communities by providing affordable clothing, strengthening livelihoods, and contributing to the global goals of sustainable development (SDGs 8, 12, and 13).
